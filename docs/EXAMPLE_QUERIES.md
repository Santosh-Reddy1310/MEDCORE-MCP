## Tips for Better Queries

1. **Be Specific**: "Show me critical patients in ICU" is better than "Show me patients"
2. **Use Filters**: Include ward, status, or department when relevant
3. **Ask for Insights**: "Which ward is most crowded?" instead of just "Show me wards"
4. **Combine Data**: "Show me available doctors and free beds" for operational planning
5. **Use Natural Language**: The AI understands conversational queries

## Expected Response Format

The AI will typically respond with:
- **Summary**: Key findings or answer to your question
- **Data**: Relevant patient/doctor/bed information
- **Insights**: Analysis or recommendations
- **Next Steps**: Suggested follow-up actions if applicable
